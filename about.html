History of our locations

Our Journey Began in Leeds

In the heart of Leeds, our story began with a single cafe. We embarked on a mission to create a unique and unforgettable experience for our customers. Over two years, we refined our craft, listened to our patrons, and became a beloved part of the community.


Expanding to Harrogate

With one successful cafe behind us, we were ready to take the next step. That's when we ventured into the charming town of Harrogate. Our second cafe marked a new chapter in our journey, as we shared our passion for exceptional food and service with the people of Harrogate.


A Historic Landmark: Knareborough Castle

But our journey was far from over. Just six months later, we achieved a remarkable milestone by acquiring a location in Knaresborough Castle. This historic site offered a unique opportunity for us to continue our mission in an iconic and unforgettable setting.

Today, our story continues to unfold, and we invite you to be a part of it. Join us in these beautiful locations, each with its own unique charm and history, and let us create memorable moments together.
